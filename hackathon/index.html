Orange

Juicy product review goodness based on machine learning. A product of HackMIT.

Co-conspirators: Drew Macneil and Brian To.

Skip the Pulp

Product reviews done right

Orange is an automated product review aggregator which automatically and intelligently analyzes reviews and extracts key facts from the things you want to buy from trusted sources and communities. Powered by the AlchemyAPI cloud and available on both iOS and web, Orange enables you to get structured, glanceable information on a product's pros and cons, as well as an overall rating based on aggregate sentiment from online communities and forums. The iOS app even includes a barcode reader, for added convenience and ease of use. So skip the pulp; use Orange and get only the product info you really need.

Curtain

Truly social payments. A product of MasterCard N>XT.

Co-conspirators: Drew Macneil and Brian To.

Shop Smarter

More than just a mobile wallet

Think about the transactions you make in your everyday life: coffees, lunches, etc. Pretty mundane, right? We believe these purchases have extraordinary and unrealized potential with regards to sharing, trends, and offers. Curtain is more than just a mobile wallet. Effortlessly share purchases with your friends, and over time, the app starts to help you. Keep tabs on your spending, get personalized offers, and shop smarter than ever with Curtain.

Drive

Next-gen media controls. A product of The Next 36 Hackathon.

Partner in crime: Drew Macneil.

Take Control

Use ALL the gestures

Drive is a media player made for the long haul. The UI is completely gesture-driven, allowing for nearly sightless navigation. Instead of fumbling to view your phone's media controls, simply swipe and tap anywhere onscreen. Additionally, when paired with Thalmic Labs' Myo, gesture control is activated, enabling you to control your phone without even touching it. So what are you waiting for? Get Drive and get going.
